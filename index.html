Welcome to BrawlPedia!

BrawlPedia is an open-source discord application used for collecting, trading & unlocking collectibles named brawlers.

Based off of the Supercell game Brawl Stars, many different characters & items can be seen across many of the features inside of BrawlPedia!

This bot is also inspired by Ballsdex, a discord bot used for the same purpose, but with colletibles named Countryballs.

You can obtain these brawlers by chatting in a discord server that has the app installed and configured in a certain channel by an admin.

Once the brawler spawns, click the red "Catch this brawler!" button and see if you can guess the name! If you are stumped,

you can use the gray hint button for some name, rarity or ability hints.

Look out, though, some people are wicked fast, you never know if you're able to catch the brawler or not!

Once the brawler is caught, and you have gotten pinged by the bot, congrats! You have successfully obtained your first (if not another) brawler!

Depending on it's chances of spawning (rarity), you should have gotten a certain amount of Brawl Pass XP! Once you catch enough brawlers,

you can automatically level up to a new brawl pass level! Each level requires more and more XP. View your progress with /brawlpass progress.

You can view a brawlers card by using /brawlers info and selecting the brawler you would like to view. If you have many,

just type in its name in the text box. (e.g. brawler:Darryl). If you have multiple Darryl's, just choose the one at the top!

Once selected, send the command and then the bot will send the card along with other details!

You can see your last caught brawler with /brawlers last as well, this is very useful!

Another way to obtain brawlers is by opening BOXES, a daily feature that gives you boxes of brawlers determined by chance.

You can open a box by using the /open_box command. Once ran, you will either get a Small Box (49%), Big Box (35%), Mega Box (15%) or Omega Box (1%).

Each give 2, 4, 6, and 10 brawlers. Omega Boxes also have a chance to give you the infamous Omega Box Darryl,

which is a 1/25 chance from 1 mega box! How insane! Once you have opened your box, you have to wait 1 day to open one again!
