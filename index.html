What even is BrawlPedia?

BrawlPedia is a Discord Bot that revolves around the Brawl Stars mobile game. When configured with the /config channel command, it can spawn a range of brawlers and skins that are able to be caught via clicking the red "Catch this brawler!" button and typing their name! Some brawlers can have special "fame" cards, ranging from global to alien fame.

Another way of obtaining brawlers is by opening boxes using the /open_box command. There are 4 kinds of boxes: Small, Big, Mega, and Omega — each with a chance of 49%, 35%, 15%, and 1% respectively.

Brawlers & skins of certain rarities also carry different amounts of Brawl Pass XP, which is automatically stored into your brawl pass. Once you achieve a certain amount of the XP, you progress to a new level! Levels can help you well, uh, I really don't know yet, but its a work in progress!
