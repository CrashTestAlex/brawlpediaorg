What even is BrawlPedia?

BrawlPedia is a Discord Bot that revolves around the Brawl Stars mobile game.

When configured with the /config channel command, it can spawn brawlers and skins that players can catch by typing their name.

Some brawlers can have special “fame” cards, ranging from global to alien fame.

Another way of obtaining brawlers is by opening boxes with the /open_box command.

There are 4 kinds of boxes: Small (49%), Big (35%), Mega (15%), and Omega (1%).

Brawlers & skins contribute to Brawl Pass XP, which levels up your Brawl Pass automatically.
